~Recipe~
Heat oil in a pan or kadhai over medium heat. Add mustard seeds and let them splutter. Then add cumin seeds and asafoetida.
Add chopped onions, green chilies, ginger, and garlic. Saut√© until onions turn translucent.
Add chopped tomatoes and cook until they turn mushy.
Add turmeric powder, coriander powder, cumin powder, red chili powder, and salt. Mix well and cook for 2-3 minutes.
Add chopped cauliflower florets and green peas. Mix well and cook for another 5-7 minutes until the vegetables are partially cooked.
Add mashed potatoes and mix well. Add a little water if the mixture is too dry. Cook for another 5-7 minutes until all the vegetables are completely cooked and the flavors are well combined.
Once the bhaji is ready, garnish with fresh coriander leaves and keep it aside.
For Pav, slit the pav horizontally but not all the way through.
Heat a tawa (griddle) or a pan. Add a little butter and toast the pav on both sides until golden brown and crispy.
Serve the hot and spicy bhaji with butter-toasted pav along with a slice of lemon and some chopped onions on the side.
Enjoy the delicious Bhaji Pav hot!
