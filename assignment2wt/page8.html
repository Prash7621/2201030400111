~Recipe~
Boil Water: Bring 2 cups of water to a boil in a saucepan.
Add Noodles: Break the Maggi noodles into smaller pieces and add them to the boiling water. Cook for about 2-3 minutes or until the noodles are soft, stirring occasionally to prevent sticking.
Add Vegetables: If using, add chopped vegetables to the boiling noodles. You can use a variety of vegetables such as carrots, peas, bell peppers, and onions. Cook for an additional 2-3 minutes until the vegetables are tender.
Seasoning: Once the noodles and vegetables are cooked, add the Maggi masala seasoning provided in the packet. Stir well to combine and evenly distribute the seasoning.
Optional Protein: If desired, add cooked chicken, shrimp, or tofu to the noodles for added protein. Mix well to incorporate.
Serve: Once everything is heated through and well combined, remove from heat and transfer the Maggi noodles to serving bowls.
Garnish: Optionally, garnish with chopped fresh coriander leaves or a drizzle of chili sauce or ketchup for extra flavor.
Enjoy: Serve hot and enjoy your delicious Maggi noodles!
