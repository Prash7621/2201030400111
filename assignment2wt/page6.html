~Recipe~
Prepare Potato Filling: Heat oil in a pan. Add mustard seeds and let them splutter. Add cumin seeds, asafoetida, chopped green chilies, grated ginger, and minced garlic. Sauté until fragrant.
Add curry leaves and sauté for a few seconds. Then add turmeric powder, red chili powder, and mashed potatoes. Mix well to combine all the ingredients. Add salt to taste and chopped coriander leaves. Cook for 2-3 minutes. Remove from heat and let it cool.
Shape and Fry Vadas: Divide the potato mixture into equal-sized balls. Flatten each ball slightly to form a patty or vada.
Heat oil for deep frying in a kadhai or deep pan. Meanwhile, prepare the batter by mixing chickpea flour, turmeric powder, baking soda (if using), and salt. Add water gradually to form a smooth, lump-free batter with a thick consistency.
Dip each potato patty into the batter, ensuring it's evenly coated. Carefully drop it into the hot oil and fry until golden brown and crispy. Remove and drain excess oil on paper towels.
Assemble Vada Pav: Slit the pav horizontally but not all the way through. Spread green chutney on one side and tamarind chutney on the other.
Place a hot vada in the center and press lightly. Optionally, sprinkle some dry garlic chutney on top for extra flavor.
Serve hot with sliced onions and more chutney on the side.
Enjoy the delicious and iconic Vada Pav!
